## Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist (For College Students in India)

### 🔐 General Online Safety
- Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts and enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading files from unknown sources
- Always verify website URLs before entering personal or login information
- Keep your devices and apps updated to protect against security vulnerabilities
- Do not share personal information (address, phone number, ID details) on public platforms

### 💳 Financial & UPI Safety
- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone under any circumstances
- Do not approve “collect requests” unless you are certain about the sender
- Remember: entering your UPI PIN is only required to SEND money, not receive it
- Avoid scanning random QR codes, especially from unknown or untrusted sources

### 📱 Social Media & Communication Safety
- Adjust privacy settings to control who can view your posts and personal information
- Be cautious of messages from unknown contacts posing as friends, seniors, or authorities
- Do not engage in or respond to suspicious job offers, giveaways, or urgent financial requests

### 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime (India)
- Report incidents immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 for urgent financial fraud cases
